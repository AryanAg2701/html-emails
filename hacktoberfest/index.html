Hacktoberfest 2024 is here!

Hello Everyone,

We hope you're doing well!

Hacktoberfest 2024 is in full swing, and there's still plenty of time to dive in and contribute! Whether you're a pro developer or just starting out, this is a great opportunity to get involved with the open-source community and make meaningful contributions throughout October.

What’s Hacktoberfest?

Hacktoberfest is a global event celebrating open-source, hosted by DigitalOcean. It encourages developers of all skill levels to contribute to open-source repositories on GitHub or GitLab. It's the perfect chance to gain hands-on experience, collaborate with others, and showcase your work.

How You Can Get Involved

  • Sign Up Now: Register on the Hacktoberfest website to participate.
  • Submit Pull Requests: Contribute to open-source projects by submitting pull requests (PRs) that improve code, fix bugs, enhance documentation, or add new features. Every accepted contribution counts!
  • Earn a Digital Badge: Hacktoberfest participants will receive a customizable digital badge through Holopin, representing your contributions and progress in the event.

How We, Lambda, Are Supporting You

We understand that contributing to open-source can be a bit overwhelming, but we’re here to help:

  • We’ve tagged the IITH Dashboard App with Hacktoberfest, so you can contribute to it as part of the challenge! This is a project that directly impacts our community, and your contributions will help make it better.
  • We’ll be providing resources, beginner-friendly issues, and guidance throughout the rest of the month.
  • If you need help finding projects or understanding the process of submitting a PR, feel free to reach out to us!

Why Should You Participate?

Here are some great reasons to get involved:

  • Learn New Skills: Enhance your coding and problem-solving abilities.
  • Collaborate Globally: Work with developers from around the world.
  • Earn Exclusive Holopin Badges: Showcase your contributions with special badges.

There’s still time to contribute and make a difference.
